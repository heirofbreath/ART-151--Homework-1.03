    "DALLAS" ( presumed to be operating under the alias "NATHAN STEELE" ) serves as the gang's effective leader, second only to their ringleader, "BAIN". A former member of the Chicago mob, "DALLAS" is extremely cunning and experienced, leaving with a target on his head after attempting to turn two mob families against each other. After living life on the run as an unliscensed physician, Steele remerged from the criminal underworld as one of the four original members of the PAYDAY crew.
